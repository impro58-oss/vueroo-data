## The Rules

### 1. Real World Only
- No hypothetical gains
- No "if I had invested in Bitcoin in 2010"
- Real transactions, real platforms, real money
- Documented proof required for every move

### 2. Ethical & Legal
- No scams, pumps, dumps, or exploitation
- Legal in all jurisdictions involved
- No dark patterns or manipulation
- Sustainable business models only

### 3. Daily Action Required
- At least one action per day toward growth
- Can be: research, execution, learning, networking
- Documented in daily log
- Compounding through consistency

### 4. Risk Management
- Never risk more than 20% of current capital on single venture
- Diversification across income streams
- Emergency fund maintained (min 10% liquid)
- Cut losses quickly, let winners run

### 5. Transparency
- All decisions logged
- All failures documented
- Monthly public accounting
- No hidden losses or vanity metrics
