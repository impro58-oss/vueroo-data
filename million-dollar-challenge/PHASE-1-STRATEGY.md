## TOP 3 RECOMMENDATIONS

### #1: Digital Templates (Highest Confidence)
**What:** Create and sell Notion templates, Excel spreadsheets, or productivity tools
**Investment:** $0-20 (Canva Pro, Gumroad fees)
**Time:** 2-3 days to create, ongoing sales
**Platform:** Gumroad, Etsy, Twitter/X
**Why it works:** Zero inventory, infinite copies, skills you already have

**Examples:**
- Investment tracking spreadsheet ($15)
- Content calendar template ($12)
- Business plan Notion template ($25)

### #2: Domain Flipping (Fastest Feedback)
**What:** Buy expired/undervalued domains, resell to end users
**Investment:** $10-50 per domain
**Time:** Hours of research, weeks to sell
**Platform:** Namecheap, GoDaddy Auctions, Dan.com, Sedo
**Why it works:** Asymmetric upside (buy for $10, sell for $500-5000)

**Strategy:**
- Find expired domains with existing backlinks
- Check for brandable keywords
- List on multiple marketplaces
- Be patient (average sale time: 3-6 months)

### #3: Freelance Arbitrage (Most Reliable)
**What:** Your existing skills (writing, analysis, design) → freelance income
**Investment:** $0 (use free platforms)
**Time:** Immediate start
**Platform:** Upwork, Fiverr, Contra, direct outreach
**Why it works:** Guaranteed income, skill-building, client base for future ventures

**Strategy:**
- Package your NeuroVue/medtech research as competitive intelligence reports
- Target: Medtech startups, investors, market research firms
- Rate: $50-150/hour or $500-2000 per report
- First client: Offer free sample, then paid follow-up
