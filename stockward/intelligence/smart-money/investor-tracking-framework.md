## 📊 DATA SOURCES — Where the Footprints Live

### Tier 1: Real-Time Deal Tracking

#### **PitchBook** (Gold Standard)
**URL:** https://pitchbook.com/  
**Cost:** $$$ (Expensive, but comprehensive)  
**What It Tracks:**
- Every VC/PE deal globally
- Investor profiles and portfolio companies
- Round sizes, valuations, co-investors
- Emerging managers and first-time funds

**How to Use (Free Alternatives):**
- Subscribe to newsletters
- Follow Twitter/X accounts that scrape PitchBook
- Use public press releases

**Key Signal:** "Who invested WITH whom" (co-investor patterns)

---

#### **Crunchbase** (Free Tier Available)
**URL:** https://www.crunchbase.com/  
**Cost:** Free (basic), $$$ (Pro with alerts)  
**What It Tracks:**
- Startup funding rounds
- Investor profiles
- Trending sectors
- Emerging unicorns

**How to Use:**
- Set up alerts for sectors you're tracking
- Follow investor profiles (see their portfolio)
- Track "Trending" companies by sector
- Export data for analysis

**Key Signal:** Clustering in specific sectors/geographies

---

#### **CB Insights** (Newsletter Free)
**URL:** https://www.cbinsights.com/research/  
**Cost:** Free newsletters, $$$ for full platform  
**What It Tracks:**
- Market maps (who's who in each sector)
- Investor rankings (top VCs by sector)
- State of [Industry] reports
- Unicorn tracker

**Key Signal:** Market map updates (new entrants, recent funding)

### Tier 3: Regulatory and Public Filings

#### **SEC EDGAR Database** (US Public Companies)
**URL:** https://www.sec.gov/edgar  
**What It Tracks:**
- 13F filings (institutional investor holdings)
- Form D (private placement notices)
- Beneficial ownership reports

**How to Use:**
- Track which public companies top funds are buying
- See sector rotations (healthcare → tech → energy)
- Identify new positions (early signals)

**Key Signal:** 13F filing changes (quarterly institutional holdings)

---

#### **Form D Filings** (Private Company Signals)
**URL:** https://www.sec.gov/edgar/search/  
**What It Tracks:**
- Every private company raising capital in US
- Amount raised, investors, date

**Key Signal:** Velocity of fundraising in specific sectors

| **International expansion (US → EU/Asia)** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Global trend |

---

### Step 3: Synergy Mapping

**The Hidden Connections:**

```
Investor A → Company X (AI Infrastructure)
         ↘ Company Y (AI Healthcare)
         ↘ Company Z (AI Finance)
         
Investor B → Company Y (AI Healthcare)
         ↘ Company W (AI Drug Discovery)
         
SYNERGY DETECTED: AI + Healthcare convergence
THESIS: Infrastructure → Application → Outcome
```

**Pattern Recognition:**
- **Vertical Stacking:** Same investor betting across supply chain
- **Thematic Clustering:** Multiple funds betting on same macro trend
- **Geographic Spread:** Same sector, multiple regions (global trend)
- **Follow-on Behavior:** Who follows whom (lead/lag relationships)

---

### Step 4: Timing the Wave

**Early Stage (Best for Entrepreneurs):**
- Seed/Series A activity
- Angel syndicates forming
- First institutional checks
- **Action:** Build, validate, get ready

**Growth Stage (Market Validation):**
- Series B/C mega-rounds
- Unicorn creation
- **Action:** Scale fast, capture market

**Late Stage (Trend Confirmed):**
- Pre-IPO rounds
- PE buyouts
- **Action:** Optimize, exit, or hold

**The Sweet Spot:** Series A/B activity — trend validated, market not crowded yet
