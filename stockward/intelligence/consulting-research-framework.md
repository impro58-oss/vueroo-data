## 🎯 FRAMEWORK — How to Extract Value

### Step 1: Trend Detection
**What to look for:**
- [ ] Growth rates >20% CAGR mentioned
- [ ] "Billion-dollar market" references
- [ ] Technology inflection points
- [ ] Regulatory tailwinds
- [ ] Demographic shifts

**Signal Strength Scale:**
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **CONFIRMED:** Multiple sources, hard data, clear trajectory
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ **STRONG:** Cited by Big 3, some data, momentum building
- ⭐⭐⭐ **EMERGING:** Single source, directional, needs validation
- ⭐⭐ **SPECULATIVE:** Early stage, conceptual, high risk
- ⭐ **IDEA:** Mentioned in passing, no data yet

## 🔄 WORKFLOW — How We Operate

### Daily (Morning Scan — 15 minutes)
1. Check Big 3 homepages for new publications
2. Scan RSS feeds for keywords (AI, biotech, fintech, energy)
3. Skim abstracts, flag promising reports
4. Save URLs to `sources/[firm]-index.md`

### Weekly (Deep Dive — 2 hours)
1. Read flagged reports in full
2. Extract data points and insights
3. Fill opportunity templates
4. Update synthesis matrix
5. Identify top 3 opportunities for further research

### Monthly (Strategic Review — 4 hours)
1. Review all identified opportunities
2. Validate with external sources (industry experts, VC reports)
3. Score opportunities (market size, timing, competition, fit)
4. Select 1-2 to pursue for deeper due diligence
5. Archive weak signals, double down on strong ones
