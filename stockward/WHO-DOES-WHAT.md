## 🚨 Escalation Triggers

Escalate to full leadership discussion when:
- Equity ask outside 0.25%–5% range
- Sector outside medtech/software core expertise
- Conflict of interest with existing portfolio
- Timeline pressure < 48 hours
- Founder requests exclusivity or accelerates timeline
- Deal size exceeds comfort threshold
- Regulatory or legal red flags emerge
