**Critical Insight:** Platform conversion rate is the highest-leverage variable. A 5% vs. 20% penetration difference creates a **4x revenue swing** ‚Äî more important than market growth or pricing.

## Key Risks to SOM Achievement

### Risk 1: Conversion Rate Collapse (HIGH IMPACT)
**Scenario:** Despite market growth, EDUCO AI only captures 3‚Äì5% of homeschoolers due to:
- Trust deficit (AI teaching kids triggers safety concerns)
- Competition (Khan Academy + AI, Synthesis, etc.)
- Free tier cannibalization (no upgrade path)

**Mitigation:** 
- SEN-first GTM (highest willingness to pay, lowest alternatives)
- Heavy investment in parent education and trust-building
- Certification partnerships as credibility shortcut

### Risk 2: Regulatory Freeze (MEDIUM IMPACT)
**Scenario:** Major state(s) restrict homeschooling or require state curriculum alignment, shrinking addressable market 20‚Äì30%.

**States to Watch:** California, New York, Massachusetts (restrictive tendencies)

**Mitigation:**
- Position as "supplemental" not "replacement" in early messaging
- UAE/Estonia proof-of-concept before US scale
- Legal defense fund earmarked

### Risk 3: Pricing Compression (MEDIUM IMPACT)
**Scenario:** Big Tech (Google/Microsoft) offers comparable AI tutoring for free; EDUCO AI forced to compete at $50‚Äì80/mo instead of $120‚Äì300/mo.

**Mitigation:**
- Double down on certification layer (Big Tech won't touch regulatory complexity)
- Human mentor network as differentiator (high touch, defensible)
- B2B institutional contracts (less price-sensitive than consumers)

### Risk 4: Churn Avalanche (HIGH IMPACT)
**Scenario:** Parents try homeschooling, find it unsustainable, return to traditional school. EDUCO AI sees >20% monthly churn, LTV:CAC ratio collapses.

**Mitigation:**
- Community features (peer connection reduces isolation)
- Hybrid "school support" tier (not full replacement)
- Mentorship as retention hook (human relationships increase switching costs)

## Validation Checklist

Before claiming $1.4B SOM is achievable, verify:

- [ ] **Customer Acquisition:** Can we acquire homeschool families at CAC < $200?
- [ ] **Conversion Proof:** Do 10%+ of free tier users upgrade to paid within 12 months?
- [ ] **Retention:** Is annual churn < 25% (monthly < 2.5%)?
- [ ] **Certification Traction:** Do 3+ universities accept EDUCO AI credentials by Year 3?
- [ ] **Unit Economics:** Is LTV:CAC > 3:1 at scale?
- [ ] **Regulatory Stability:** Are top 10 states (by homeschool population) legally stable?

**If 4+ boxes checked:** Optimistic case probable  
**If 2‚Äì3 boxes checked:** Base case likely  
**If <2 boxes checked:** Bear case ‚Äî reconsider opportunity
