# FINTENDANT — 5-Year Vertical-to-Platform Roadmap
**From Financial Agent → Agent Economy Platform**
**Date:** March 24, 2026  
**Status:** Strategic Planning Document  
**Vision:** Build the definitive AI agent for finance, then expand to become the trust layer for the broader agent economy

---

## Executive Summary

**The Strategy:** Start narrow (finance), deepen (full financial life), then broaden (adjacent domains) to become a horizontal platform.

**Why This Works:**
1. **Finance is trust-heavy** — Master here, users will trust you elsewhere
2. **Measurable outcomes** — $ saved is clear value, builds brand
3. **Data moat** — Financial patterns are hard to replicate
4. **Regulatory clarity** — Navigable vs. undefined "general agent" regulations
5. **Expandable** — Adjacent verticals (insurance, taxes, legal) share infrastructure

**Timeline:** 5 years to horizontal platform dominance
**Capital Required:** $50-75M across 5 years
**Success Probability:** 30-35% (vs. 10% for horizontal-first)

---

## Year-by-Year Roadmap

### YEAR 1 (2026) — PROVE TRUST
**Theme:** "Can users delegate money to AI?"

#### Objectives
| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Users** | 50,000 paying | Validate demand exists |
| **Trust Rate** | 30% grant execution authority | Prove trust model works |
| **Savings/User** | $200+ annually | Demonstrate clear value |
| **CAC** | <$100 | Unit economics viable |
| **Churn** | <30% | Product-market fit signal |
| **Revenue** | $15M ARR | Self-funding foundation |

#### Product Focus
**Core Features:**
- Credit card optimization (rewards maximization)
- Subscription management (identify & cancel unused)
- Basic bank account optimization (HYSA transfers)
- Deal hunting (cashback, price protection)

**Trust Features:**
- Transparent audit trail (every action logged)
- Spending caps (user-defined limits)
- Pause button (instant stop)
- Weekly reports (what AI did, why)

| Risk | Mitigation | Owner |

## Why This Roadmap Wins

### 1. Risk-Adjusted Returns
- **Vertical-first:** 30-35% success rate, $4-6B outcome
- **Horizontal-first:** 10% success rate, $10B+ outcome (if it works)
- **Expected value:** Vertical ($1.4B) > Horizontal ($1B)

### 2. Each Year Validates the Next
- Year 1 proves trust model → justifies Year 2 expansion
- Year 2 proves scalability → justifies Year 3 verticals
- Year 3 proves expandability → justifies Year 4 platform
- Year 4 proves platform → justifies Year 5 dominance

### 3. Capital Efficient
- Self-funding by Year 2 ($80M ARR)
- Profitable by Year 3 ($40M EBITDA)
- No desperation rounds

### 4. Defensible Position
- Data moat: 5 years of financial optimization patterns
- Trust moat: "FINTENDANT saved me $5,000"
- Network effects: More users → better AI → more savings → more users
