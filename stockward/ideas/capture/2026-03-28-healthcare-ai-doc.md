## Initial Framing

**Problem:** Clinical documentation is a massive time sink for healthcare professionals, done manually hours after patient interactions, reconstructed from memory, prone to errors and delays.  
**Solution:** Real-time AI voice recognition that converts ward rounds and clinical interactions into structured medical notes automatically.  
**Market:** NHS UK + global healthcare systems. Administrative burden in healthcare is 30%+ of costs.  
**Differentiation:** Built by clinician who understands workflow vs. generic transcription tools. Integration with NHS systems.
