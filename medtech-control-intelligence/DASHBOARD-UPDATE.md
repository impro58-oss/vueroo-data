## How to Use New Features

### Product Selection
- Window now shows **8 products at once** (all 14 visible with minimal scrolling)
- Hold Ctrl and click to select multiple specific products
- Click "All Products" to reset

### Country Selection
1. **Select a Region** from dropdown → Countries auto-populate
2. Use **checkboxes** to fine-tune individual countries
3. Click **✓ Select All** to check all countries in the region
4. Click **✕ Clear All** to uncheck all (then select specific ones)
5. Charts update automatically with each change
