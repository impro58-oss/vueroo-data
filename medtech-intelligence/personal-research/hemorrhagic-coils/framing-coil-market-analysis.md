## Framing Coil Segment Analysis

### What Defines the Framing Coil Segment?

Framing coils are specifically designed for the **initial deployment** in aneurysm embolization:
- Create the initial stable basket/framework
- 3D or complex shapes with variable diameter loops
- Stiffer than filling/finishing coils
- **One framing coil per procedure** (typically)
- Higher price point due to complex geometry

### Clinical Usage Patterns

**Key Finding from Clinical Literature:**
- **64.65%** of coiling procedures use framing first coil approach
- Typical aneurysm procedure uses **3-11+ coils total**:
  - 1 framing coil (3D/Complex)
  - 2-8 filling coils (standard/soft)
  - 1-2 finishing coils (ultra-soft/nano)

**Procedure Sequence:**
1. Framing coil → stabilizes the structure
2. Filling coils → pack the basket (2-3+ typically)
3. Finishing coils → dense pack the neck

## Growth Drivers for Framing Coil Segment

### Positive Factors:
1. **Aging population** → more aneurysm cases
2. **Shift toward coiling vs clipping** → more endovascular procedures
3. **Complex aneurysm treatments** → premium framing coils
4. **Hydrogel framing coils** (HydroFrame) → higher ASP
5. **Flow diversion + coiling** → hybrid procedures

### Risk Factors:
1. **Flow diversion growth** → may reduce coil-only procedures
2. **Price pressure** from hospitals/payers
3. **Generic/bare platinum competition** → commoditization
4. **Single-coil per procedure** → volume capped by procedure count

## Key Insights

### 1. Framing Coils Are Premium Products
- Command 2-3x price of standard filling coils
- Complex geometry justifies premium positioning
- Always the first coil deployed = strategic importance

### 2. Market Concentrated Among Top 3
- 75-90% market share controlled by Stryker, Medtronic, MicroVention
- High barriers to entry (regulatory, clinical evidence)
- Proprietary 3D shapes are differentiated IP

### 3. Growth Limited by Procedure Volume
- One framing coil per procedure = volume capped
- Growth driven by:
  - Procedure volume increase (aging demographics)
  - ASP increase (hydrogel, advanced designs)
  - Market share shifts

### 4. Hydrogel Framing = Emerging Premium Segment
- MicroVention's HydroFrame unique in market
- 4-9x volume expansion = fewer coils needed
- Trade-off: higher per-coil cost vs fewer total coils

### 5. Clinical Preference Drives Share
- Physician familiarity with specific 3D shapes
- Deployment predictability crucial
- Long-term occlusion data matters
