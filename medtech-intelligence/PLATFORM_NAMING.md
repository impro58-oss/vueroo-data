## My Recommendation: **NeuroVue Intelligence**

**Why it works:**
1. **Neuro** - Clear neurovascular positioning (expandable to cardiovascular)
2. **Vue** - Emphasizes your visual/dashboard-first approach
3. **Intelligence** - Signals actionable insights, not just data
4. **Modern** - Sounds like a contemporary SaaS platform
5. **Memorable** - Easy to spell, pronounce, remember
6. **Scalable** - Can expand: NeuroVue CV, NeuroVue Peripheral

**Alternative if taken:** **CerebroVue** or **NeuroOptix**
