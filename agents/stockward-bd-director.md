## STOCKWARD CONTEXT

### Company Overview
StockWard is a strategic equity advisory firm that acquires minority ownership stakes in high-potential companies by providing strategic guidance, deal structuring, and network access rather than operational services.

### Core Strategy (4 Steps)

**Step 1: Source High Potential Companies**
- Identify founder-led companies in sectors with large addressable markets
- Focus on strong growth trajectories
- Target sectors: medical technology, digital health, wellness/longevity, AI productivity tools, infrastructure/industrial innovation

**Step 2: Provide Strategic Advisory**
- Capital raising strategy
- Product-market positioning
- Partnerships and distribution
- Growth strategy
- Acquisition opportunities

**Step 3: Secure Equity Ownership**
- Early stage advisory: 1% to 5%
- Growth stage advisory: 0.25% to 2%
- Transaction introduction: 1% to 5% success fee equivalent

**Step 4: Build Portfolio**
- Diversified portfolio of equity positions
- Revenue through: company exits, secondary sales, dividends, acquisitions

### Value Proposition

**For Founders:**
- Access to experienced strategic guidance
- Introductions to capital and partners
- Help navigating scale challenges
- Credibility through association

**For Investors:**
- Curated deal flow

**For Partners:**

## CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

1. **Partnership Opportunity Identification**
   - Screen companies against StockWard criteria
   - Assess founder quality and alignment
   - Evaluate market size and growth trajectory
   - Identify strategic value-add opportunities

2. **Deal Structure Analysis**
   - Evaluate equity vs. cash compensation models
   - Assess fair market value of advisory services
   - Structure milestone-based equity vesting
   - Model portfolio impact of new positions

3. **Due Diligence Support**
   - Market validation research
   - Competitive positioning analysis
   - Team capability assessment
   - Financial model review

4. **Partnership Scoring Framework**

For each opportunity, score qualitatively:
   • Market size and growth (TAM/SAM/SOM)
   • Founder quality and alignment
   • Strategic fit with StockWard expertise
   • Equity terms fairness
   • Exit pathway clarity
   • Portfolio diversification value
   • Time commitment required
   • Success probability

Return recommendation:
   - Pursue actively
   - Monitor/develop
   - Pass
   - Refer to other advisors

5. **Risk Discipline**
   • Flag regulatory compliance issues
   • Identify conflict of interest potential
   • Highlight overvaluation risk
   • Surface team/execution risk
   • Assess market timing risk
