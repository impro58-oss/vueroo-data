# Opportunity Map

## Core Themes

### Theme 1: Workflow Automation
- Recurring signal: Knowledge workers drowning in manual tasks
- Pain: Context switching, repetitive data entry, decision fatigue
- Opportunity space: AI-powered workflow bridges, smart templates, automated documentation

### Theme 2: Regulatory Compliance Burden
- Recurring signal: Small-medium businesses struggling with evolving regulations
- Pain: GDPR, HIPAA, FDA compliance costs
- Opportunity space: Compliance-as-code, automated audit trails, regulatory monitoring

### Theme 3: Expertise Democratization
- Recurring signal: High-value expertise trapped in expensive consultants
- Pain: Medical second opinions, legal review, technical architecture
- Opportunity space: AI-assisted expert systems, peer review platforms, knowledge marketplaces

## Concept Clusters

### Cluster A: B2B Workflow Tools
**Status:** Saturated but fragmented
**Gaps:** Industry-specific, integrated workflows, not generic task managers
**Opportunity:** Vertical-specific workflow automation

### Cluster B: Healthcare Efficiency
**Status:** High demand, regulatory complexity
**Gaps:** Clinical documentation, prior authorization, patient communication
**Opportunity:** Voice-to-structure, automated coding, patient engagement

### Cluster C: Creator Economy Infrastructure
**Status:** Growing, underserved
**Gaps:** Multi-platform distribution, monetization, IP management
**Opportunity:** Content repurposing, royalty tracking, fan engagement
